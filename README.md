## Abdallah Dalis

Empirical economist in Chicago. M.S. Economics & Quantitative Analysis, DePaul University, Kellstadt Graduate School of Business (2026, With Distinction); B.S. Mathematics & Computer Science (UIC). My research asks one question: when the economy takes a hit, who actually pays? I trace how aggregate shocks and market structures land unevenly across counties, neighborhoods, and households.

**Now:** publishing my monetary-incidence research program and seeking predoctoral / research assistant roles in economics. Previously: Graduate Research Assistant at DePaul (firm-level panels, LLM-assisted data pipelines); Economic & Data Analyst Intern at the Chicago Transit Authority.
